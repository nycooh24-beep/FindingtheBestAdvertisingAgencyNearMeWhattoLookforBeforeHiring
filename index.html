About Ooh NYC

Ooh NYC is a leading authority in out-of-home advertising, dedicated to delivering powerful and innovative campaigns that reach audiences beyond the confines of their homes. We specialize in creating unforgettable advertising solutions that capture attention wherever people go, ensuring your brand remains at the forefront of their minds.

From Times Square to Brooklyn streets, we've helped countless brands—from startups to Fortune 500 companies—create memorable outdoor advertising campaigns that drive real results.

  • Premium citywide billboard advertising (static & digital displays)
  • High-traffic street-level & storefront media placements
  • Mobile billboard truck campaigns across all five boroughs
  • Complete OOH strategy, creative design, and performance analytics
  • 24/7 customer support and campaign monitoring
  • Flexible packages tailored to your budget and goals

With years of experience in the outdoor advertising industry, we understand the pulse of New York City. Our strategic approach combines creativity, data-driven insights, and prime locations to maximize your brand's visibility and impact.
